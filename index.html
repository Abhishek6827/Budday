Pehli Baar

Pehli baar jab tu mili, Mujhe laga bas ek aur stranger hai. Lekin waqt ne dikhaya, Ki tu meri sabse khaas dost ban jayegi. Game mein awaaz se shuru hui, Baatein phir screen ke paar gayi. Same dreams, same hustle, same junoon, Tu samajh gayi meri har khamoshi ka matlab. BGMI ke har match mein tu saath thi, Har chicken dinner mein teri hasi. Doston ki jealousy toh hoti rahi, Lekin hum sirf ek dusre pe marte rahe. Aaj jab bhi sochta hoon us din ke baare mein, 9 March — jab kuch alag sa shuru hua tha. Anshika, tu sirf ek name nahi hai, Tu woh feeling hai jo har din nayi lagti hai.

Jab tu quiet thi, maine suna tha, Jab tu dull thi, maine roshan kiya tha. Self-respect seekhaya, clarity di, Aur tune khud se pyaar karna seekha, yahi meri jeet thi. My Gift To You

Happy Birthday, Anshika

Aaj tera special din hai, aur main bas yeh kehna chahta hoon ki tu meri life ka sabse unexpected aur sabse khoobsurat chapter hai. 9 March se lekar aaj tak, har ek moment ne mujhe samjhaya ki pyaar kya hota hai — woh bhi bina kisi formal shuruaat ke. Main paise wala nahi hoon, expensive gifts nahi de sakta. Lekin main apne haathon se kuch bana sakta hoon, apne words se tujh special feel kara sakta hoon. Yeh website sirf code nahi hai, yeh meri feelings ka ek safar hai jo maine tere liye likha hai. Tu jab dull thi, maine tujhe suna. Jab overthink karti thi, maine guide kiya. Aur tune khud se pyaar karna seekha — yeh meri sabse badi achievement hai. Aaj tu meri hai, organically, naturally. Koi pressure nahi, bas ek dusre ki understanding. Tera har birthday main aise hi celebrate karna chahta hoon — apne tareeke se, apne efforts se. Kyunki tu deserve karti hai ki koi tere liye mehnat kare, bina kisi reason ke, sirf pyaar mein. Happy Birthday, meri Anshika. Yeh din tera hai, aur main hamesha tere saath hoon — game mein, life mein, har jagah.
