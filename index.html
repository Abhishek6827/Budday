Our Story

How one game turned two strangers into each other's closest...

9 March

The First Encounter

One game, two strangers. I still remember the first time we met — something felt different. There was distance, hesitation, but also an unknown connection that I only truly understood later.

Early March

Same World, Same Dreams

Somehow we discovered we shared the same career field. Conversations grew deeper. Topics changed — from games to real life, we started talking about dreams, struggles, and everything in between.

BGMI Days

Chickens & Jealousy

Playing BGMI together, earning chicken dinners, and even navigating small jealousies helped build our trust. Friends noticed, but we only had eyes for each other.

12 March

The Friend Request

On March 12, you sent a friend request. From that day, every single day was consistent — messages, calls, shared moments. The connection wasn't just about the game anymore; it was reaching our hearts.

The Quiet Phase

When She Went Silent

A phase came when you went quiet and seemed dull. I just listened, understood, and gently guided you. We talked about self-respect and emotional clarity. And then, you made your own beautiful decision.

Today

Us — Naturally

No formal proposal, no pressure. Just organically, naturally, you chose me on your own. And here we are today — teasing, overthinking, playing, and loving. Happy Birthday, my Anshika.

Happy Birthday, Anshika

Today is your special day, and I just want to say that you are the most unexpected and the most beautiful chapter of my life. From March 9 till today, every single moment has taught me what love truly is — even without any formal beginning. I am not wealthy, and I cannot give expensive gifts. But I can build something with my own hands, and make you feel special with my words. This website is not just code — it is a journey of my feelings that I wrote entirely for you. When you were feeling dull, I listened. When you overthought, I gently guided you. And you learned to love yourself — that is my biggest achievement. Today you are mine, organically, naturally. No pressure, just mutual understanding. I want to celebrate every birthday of yours like this — in my own way, with my own efforts. Because you deserve someone who works hard for you, without any reason, purely out of love. Happy Birthday, my Anshika. This day is yours, and I am always with you — in games, in life, everywhere.
